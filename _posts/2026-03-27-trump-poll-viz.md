When communicating complex poll data like this, standard bright red and green can fail in a professional or BI context. If the colors are too harsh, they clash with standard data indicators.

To solve this, I’ve adopted a sophisticated, "CPG Executive" color scheme. We use an authoritative deep slate blue for all headers and labels, pair it with a muted gold accent, and crucially, leverage Terracotta Red and Sage Green for our diverging data points.

By choosing these "earthier," less-saturated versions of traditional colors, we create a chart that looks integrated into a professional dashboard rather than like a festive "Christmas effect." It allows for nuanced gradient analysis—using lighter, alpha-masked variants of the same red and green family for "Somewhat" categories—while ensuring the primary brand aesthetic remains authoritative and easy to read.
